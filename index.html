Our Story

Once upon a time, in the vibrant city of New York, two souls destined to meet crossed paths on a bustling street. Alex, with his adventurous spirit, and Amabella, with her infectious laughter, found themselves drawn to each other on a serendipitous day that would forever change their lives.
What began as a casual encounter soon blossomed into a beautiful friendship, filled with late-night talks and spontaneous adventures through the city that never sleeps. Together, they explored hidden gems, strolled through Central Park, and shared countless dreams over coffee in quaint cafés.
As the seasons changed, so did their feelings for one another, growing deeper with each shared moment. Their love story became a tapestry of laughter, support, and unwavering companionship.
Now, as they stand on the brink of a new chapter, Alex and Amabella invite you to witness the next step in their journey. Join them as they celebrate their love and commitment on February 14 at Ritz Carlton Hotel, surrounded by the cherished friends and family who have been part of their remarkable story
